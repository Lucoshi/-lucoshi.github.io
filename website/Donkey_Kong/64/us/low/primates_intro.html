Gorilla Grab—This move allows DK to manipulate levers and activate machinery.

WEAPON:
Coconut Shooter—DK's shooter can blast coconuts at enemies.

INSTRUMENT:
Bongo Blast—DK loves to beat the wild jungle drums!
