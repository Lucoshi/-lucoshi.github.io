BIO:
Sometime the biggest surprises come in the smallest packages, and Tiny can be really surprising! Her twirling pigtails can be really dangerous and are handy for helicopter rides through the treetops.

SPECIAL MOVE:
Ponytail Twirl—Her pigtails spin like helicopter blades!

WEAPON:
Feather Bow—It shoots as straight as an arrow and hits the target with a bang.

INSTRUMENT:
Saxophone Slam—Sometimes the smallest monkeys have the biggest lungs.
