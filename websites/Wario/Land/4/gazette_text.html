WARIO LAND 4
By Our Award-Winning Movie Critic, Wario

Box-office cash registers will be ka-chinging coast-to-coast as word hits the street about the biggest and best thriller yet from dapper action hero Wario.

Pitt's the pits and Cruise is yesterday's news but with Wario, ya got the real tough-guy deal. In WL4 the yellow-capped dynamo searches for his own private El Dorado in a pyramid hidden deep within a jungle. Armed with little more than courage and staggeringly good looks, Wario ventures into four mysterious jewel-monikered passages. Talk about screen gems!

Studio execs obviously spared no expense when it came to location shooting, taking the crew to a typhoon-lashed jungle, a haunted village and even a toxic-waste dump. Phew! The equally impressive CGI special effects include flying carpets and floating ghosts. In an industry first, producers ordered a crayon-colored set for show-stopping sequence in which Wario journeys through a child's sketch book. Color me handsome, baby!

Wario is of course the premiere cinematic artiste of his generation and in WL4 his dedication to his craft knows no bounds. When whacked by a hammer, Wario turns into a giant spring to bash through ceilings. When stung by a hovering hornet, Wario puffs up like a zeppelin and floats ceiling-ward. We're dying to find out the special FX secret behind this husky hero morphing into a tiny bat.

Crammed with thrills, chills and, lest we forget, a heart-pounding soundtrack, WL4 figures to be a lock for Best Pic at next year's Academy Awards. Meow! Tinsel Town is oh so jealous!
