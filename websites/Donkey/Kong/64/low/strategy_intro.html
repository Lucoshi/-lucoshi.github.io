It's a jungle out there, but if you want DK to get all the Golden Bananas, here's what you need to do to conquer Jungle Japes:
Swing on the vines and pound the gate switch
Collect the Banana Coins around the DK Pad
Buy DK's Coconut Shooter
Don't forget to talk to Diddy
Shoot three Coconut Switches
Get the Blueprint and give it to Snide
Buy the Baboon Blast from Cranky
Use Rambi to smash the huts and the painting of Rambi
Pound the DK Switch
Finally, use the DK Pad to reach the Barrel Blast
